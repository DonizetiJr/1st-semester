Roy Delville Roebuck (born 25 September 1929) is a British Labour Party
politician and journalist. He was Member of Parliament for Harrow East from
1966, winning the seat from the Conservative Anthony Courtney, until 1970,
when he lost the seat to the Conservative candidate Hugh Dykes.

A barrister, he married Dr Mary Ogilvy Adams . He has one son, Gavin Roebuck.
